The fundamental forces that shape your consciousness and daily experience.

Rajas

The state of motion, passion, and change. When out of balance, it manifests as restlessness, ambition, and anxiety.

Stability and Grounding

The state of inertia, darkness, and stability. Tamas provides necessary rest but can lead to lethargy and confusion when dominant.
